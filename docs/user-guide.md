# FanForge User Guide

> How to launch your fan economy in under 60 seconds

---

## Who This Is For

You're a music creator — maybe you have a TikTok audience, Audiomack streams, a loyal Telegram group. You want your superfans to feel like they're part of your journey, not just listeners. And you want a real income stream tied to that loyalty.

You don't need to know anything about crypto, blockchain, or wallets. FanForge handles all of that for you. You just talk to a bot.

---

## Getting Started

1. Open Telegram and find **@FanForgeBot**
2. Send `/start`
3. You'll see a welcome message explaining what the bot can do

That's it. No sign-up. No password. No wallet setup on your end.

---

## Step 1 — Launch Your Fan Coin

Your fan coin is a digital token your community can hold. The more fans hold it, the more engaged and invested they are in your career. Think of it like a "founding fan" badge — but one that lives on-chain and can grow in value.

**How to launch:**

Just tell FanForge what you want. You can say something like:

> "I want to launch a fan coin for my debut EP. Call it Temi Coin with the ticker TEMI."

Or simpler:

> "Launch a fan coin called VIBES"

FanForge will ask for anything it needs (a short description, maybe a cover image link). Then it handles everything:
- Links to your fan economy wallet
- Uploads your coin's info to permanent storage
- Creates the coin on Base (a fast, low-cost blockchain)
- Returns a Zora link you can share with your fans

**What you get back:**

> Your fan coin TEMI is live! Share this with your fans: https://zora.co/coin/base:0x...

Share that link anywhere — Twitter, TikTok bio, Telegram group. Fans click it and can buy and hold your coin.

## Step 2 — See Who Your Top Fans Are

Once your coin is live, fans will start holding it. You can ask FanForge to show you the rankings at any time:

> "Show me my top fans"

> "Who's holding the most TEMI?"

FanForge returns a ranked list like:

```
Your top supporters for TEMI:

1. skinnywhitegirl    — 991,109 coins (99.1%)
2. madbeets           — 8,244 coins   (0.8%)
3. 0x1f3a…c8e2        — 361 coins     (0.04%)
```

Full wallet addresses are never shown — just short handles or abbreviated addresses. Privacy by default.

## Step 3 — Create a Fan Mission

A mission gates exclusive content behind a minimum holding. If a fan holds at least X coins, they unlock your exclusive content automatically.

**Example missions:**

- "Fans holding 100+ TEMI coins get early access to my next unreleased track"
- "Anyone with 500+ coins gets an invite link to my private studio session stream"

**How to create one:**

> "Give everyone holding 100 or more TEMI coins access to this link: [your content link]"

Or:

> "Create a mission: holding 500 TEMI unlocks access to my behind-the-scenes footage at [link]"

FanForge creates the mission and asks you to confirm before distributing.

---

## Step 4 — Distribute Rewards

Once a mission is set up, tell FanForge to deliver:

> "Send the rewards out"

> "Distribute the mission now"

FanForge checks every coin holder, finds the ones who meet the threshold, and records them in the reward ledger. Each qualifying fan is marked as having received the content.

**Guarantees:**
- No fan receives the same reward twice, even if you run distribute multiple times
- You see a count: "12 fans just unlocked the content. 3 had already received it."

## Tips for Success

**Share your Zora link everywhere.** Your fan coin page on Zora is where fans discover and buy your coin. Put it in your TikTok bio, your Telegram group, your Twitter profile.

**Create missions before big moments.** A mission tied to an album drop, a livestream, or a merch collab gives fans a reason to hold more coins before the event.

**Use the recap to post.** The recap generates a Twitter post you can copy and paste. Posting consistently about your fan coin keeps the community growing.

**Don't over-explain the crypto part.** Your fans don't need to know how it works. "Hold TEMI to get early access" is the whole message.

## FAQ

**Do I need a crypto wallet?**
FanForge links to your existing Aomi wallet. Aomi handles the wallet setup when you first interact with the bot.

**What does it cost to launch a coin?**
There's a small gas fee on Base (usually under $1) for the on-chain transaction. FanForge doesn't charge anything beyond that.

**Can I change the coin after launching?**
The coin name and ticker are permanent once deployed. You can create new missions and distribute rewards at any time.

**What blockchain is this on?**
Base — a fast, low-cost Ethereum Layer 2 backed by Coinbase. Your fans don't need to know this.

**Will my coin go up in value?**
FanForge creates a Zora creator coin, which has a built-in market. Value is driven by demand from your fanbase. We can't make any predictions — but a growing, engaged community is the foundation.

**What if a fan doesn't have a crypto wallet?**
They can create one easily on [Zora.co](https://zora.co). The barrier is low, and the framing matters: "support your favourite artist and unlock exclusive content" lands much better than "buy a token."
